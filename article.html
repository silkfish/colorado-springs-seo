Colorado Springs Marketing: A Local Business Resource

Colorado Springs has a unique business climate — military presence, growing tech sector, tourism, and a rapidly growing residential population. Each segment requires a different marketing approach.

Local marketing strategy in Colorado Springs cannot be copy-pasted from Denver or any other Front Range city. The customer mix is different.

Colorado Springs marketing essentials

  • Segment your audience by Springs sub-market (military, tech, tourism, residential)
  • Use local Colorado Springs landmarks in landing pages (Garden of the Gods, Pikes Peak, downtown)
  • List in Colorado Springs Chamber and military-friendly business directories
  • Geotargeting for both Colorado Springs and unincorporated El Paso County
  • Tourism-aware content for businesses serving visitors
  • Mountain and altitude considerations for outdoor and equipment categories

The military discount factor

Roughly 1 in 5 Colorado Springs adults has a military connection (active, retired, or family). Service businesses that prominently advertise military discounts and partner with on-base resources see meaningfully higher conversion rates from this audience.
